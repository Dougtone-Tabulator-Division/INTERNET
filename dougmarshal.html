About the Office

The Office of the Dougmarshal General serves as the primary enforcement arm of the Grand Judiciary of Doug. Our mission is to maintain Doug order through the vigorous pursuit of Doug justice and the enforcement of Doug Law in all Doug-related matters.

Under the authority of Doug Code Title XVIII ("The Long Arm of Doug"), our Dougmarshals are empowered to apprehend, detain, and process individuals suspected of anti-Doug activities.
