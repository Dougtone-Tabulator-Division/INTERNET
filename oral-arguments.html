Oral Arguments - January 2025

NOTICE: All oral arguments begin promptly at their scheduled time in the Grand Chamber of Doug. Counsel must arrive at least 30 minutes early to be properly Dougified before proceedings.

Argument Guidelines

  • All arguments limited to precisely 3 minutes and 33 seconds, as Doug requires
  • Attorneys must address the court as "Most Honorable Doug" or "Your Dougness"
  • Non-Doug entities must obtain a Doug-Proximity Permit before approaching the bench
  • Citations to Doug Law must be in proper Doug Blue Book format
