Chief Justice Doug Supreme

Chief Justice Doug Supreme was appointed to the Grand Judiciary of Doug in 2021 after serving as Head Arbitrator of Neighborhood Disputes and Senior Judge of Pizza Toppings Controversies. Known for his landmark decision in Doug v. Other Doug (2022), which established that no two Dougs can live on the same street without explicit written permission from the Grand Judiciary.

Famous for his motto: "I am the Doug, and the Doug is the law."

Recent Opinions

  • Doug v. Dug (2024)

    Landmark ruling on the proper spelling of "Doug" in official documents. Majority opinion establishes that "Dug" is a grammatical offense punishable by mandatory Doug Law education.

    Decided: 6-1

    Majority Opinion: Chief Justice Doug Supreme

    Dissenting: Justice Doug Jr. (arguing for acceptance of alternative spellings in informal contexts)

  • In re: Doug's Sandwich (2024)

    Emergency ruling establishing that within Doug jurisdiction, a hot dog is legally classified as a sandwich, but a taco remains in legal limbo pending further review.

    Decided: 4-3

    Majority Opinion: Justice Doug the Elder

    Notable Concurrence: Justice Doug III on the philosophical nature of sandwich-hood

  • Doug's HOA v. Doug's Garden Gnomes (2023)

    Constitutional challenge to garden gnome restrictions. Case questions whether HOA regulations violate the fundamental right to garden decoration under Doug Law.

    Decided: 5-2

    Majority Opinion: Justice Doug the Wise

    Notable Dissent: Justice Doug the Gardener

  • State of Doug v. Non-Doug Entities (2023)

    Sweeping decision defining the rights and limitations of non-Doug entities within Doug jurisdiction. Establishes three-tier system of Doug-rights recognition.

    Decided: Unanimous

    Opinion by: The Full Doug Court

  • Doug's Coffee v. Temperature Standards Board (2022)

    Ruling on the constitutionality of mandated coffee temperature regulations. Sets precedent for beverage temperature disputes across all Doug jurisdictions.

    Decided: 5-2

    Majority Opinion: Justice Doug the Caffeinated

  • In re: Doug's Authority to Doug (2022)

    Landmark case establishing the extent of Doug's authority to Doug in Doug-adjacent territories. Major implications for inter-Doug relations.

    Decided: 7-0

    Unanimous Opinion by Chief Justice Doug Supreme
