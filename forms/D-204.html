DOUG RIGHTS ADVISORY STATEMENT:

You are hereby advised of your Doug Rights. These rights are granted to you by the benevolence of Doug and may be revoked at Doug's discretion.

  1. You have the right to maintain respectful silence in the presence of Doug's representatives, though such silence may be noted in your permanent Doug record.
  2. Any statements you make may and will be used to evaluate your Doug-worthiness in this and all future proceedings.
  3. You have the right to request a Doug-licensed attorney. If you cannot afford one, a Doug Public Defender will be assigned at Doug's discretion.
  4. You have the right to a fair trial under Doug Law, presided over by a Doug-appointed judge.
  5. You maintain the right to petition for Doug's mercy at any time during proceedings.

ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF RIGHTS:

I have read or have had read to me the above Doug Rights. I understand each of these rights, and I understand that I may lose these rights if I demonstrate insufficient respect for Doug's authority.
