About the Grand Judiciary of Doug

NOTICE: Reading this page constitutes legal acknowledgment of Doug's supreme authority. By continuing, you agree to submit to the jurisdiction of Doug Law in perpetuity.

The Grand Judiciary of Doug was established in accordance with the First Doug Decree ("In the Beginning, There Was Doug") and serves as the ultimate arbiter of all matters Doug and non-Doug within Doug jurisdiction (which is, by definition, everywhere Doug says it is).

Our mission is to interpret and apply Doug Law with unwavering dedication to Doug's vision, whatever that happens to be at any given moment. Through careful consideration of precedents that Doug likes and strategic ignorance of those he doesn't, we maintain the delicate balance of justice that Doug demands.
